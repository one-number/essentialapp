EssentialApp is an sms based lifestyle application that is routed to premium SMS short codes. The application also contains modules that are absolutely free for the subscriber.

Business Directory

An SMS based directory service application providing access to approximately 100,000 companies across Nigeria.

Cinema Schedules

Provides films and show times in Nigeria's leading cinemas. The user requests the city/cinema combination of choice and receives the entire schedule for the week.

Flight Schedules

Provides flight times between cities in Nigeria and also for international flights originating or terminating in a Nigerian city.
